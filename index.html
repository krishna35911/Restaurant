Our Story

Italian cuisine is one of the most beloved cuisines in the world, with its mix of fresh ingredients, rich sauces, and bold flavors. The history of Italian restaurants, however, goes beyond just the food. Italian restaurants have a long and fascinating history that is heavily intertwined with the immigration patterns of Italians to other countries, the rise of modern cities and urbanization, and the emergence of new technologies in the culinary world.
